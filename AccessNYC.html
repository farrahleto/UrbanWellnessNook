Access NYC Care: Your Path to Affordable Healthcare in New York City

The vibrant pulse of New York City extends beyond its skyline; it's woven into the fabric of its communities. Amidst the hustle and bustle, access to quality healthcare is essential for all New Yorkers. That's where NYC Care steps in.

NYC Care is more than just a healthcare program; it's a lifeline ensuring access to medical services without the burden of financial strain. It's designed for those who might not qualify for or afford health insurance, welcoming individuals regardless of their immigration status or financial capabilities.

How does it work? Simple. NYC Care connects you to a network of healthcare professionals across NYC Health + Hospitals locations citywide. Picture having your own doctor, tailored services, and support to keep you healthy—all at little to no cost.

Here's what you can expect: - Personalized healthcare based on what you can afford, starting at discounted fees of $0. - No membership fees, monthly costs, or premiums. - Choose your own doctor who not only treats you when you're ill but also helps you stay healthy. - Access to preventive care, mental health support, substance abuse services, and affordable prescription medications.

The process is hassle-free. Determine your eligibility, apply, and get your first appointment within two weeks. Plus, your privacy is safeguarded, ensuring enrollment won't impact your standing under the 'public charge' rule.

Eligibility is inclusive, embracing everyone from families to seniors, immigrants to those with disabilities. Getting started is a breeze—visit the NYC Care website, email, call, or find patient care sites in your community.

Your health matters, and NYC Care ensures it's accessible to all New Yorkers. Because in the city that never sleeps, your well-being never rests. Join NYC Care and pave your path to a healthier, happier you.
